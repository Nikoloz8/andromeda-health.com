    Manifesto

    As consumers, at certain moment in time, we all experienced the hurdles and drawbacks of the healthcare system. I had a privilege to experience it from both, inside (as a healthcare executive) and outside (as a patient). Opposite to most other industries, 75% of their time, consumers' (patients') here spend (waste) not on the services or products they need, but on searching, analyzing, comparing, calculating, waiting, queuing, billing and numerous red-tape. The largest sector of economy is not efficient, and it is our responsibility (of those who are concerned and capable too) to make it better by connecting the dots, building bridges of communication, expanding the bandwidth of data exchange, improving processes, automating what can be automated and, this way, ensuring smooth, fair and equal functioning of healthcare's large and complex mechanism.

    We believe that, with right technologies, innovative mind, properly set up business models and operational excellence, this is very much possible, at any scale. And this is the expertise our team brings - we have built innovative digital technologies and platforms in healthcare, we have set up proper business models and executed well in regards with the operational excellence. All combined, give us basis for faith that we can change healthcare ecosystem for good and for the benefit of all involved parties (patients, payers, providers, retailers, state and corporations).

    Great transformation requires great effort of all involved parties. That is why we bring appealing value propositions to every player in the system. We build a congruence model, in back and front, so that everyone wins as we join forces for building a new generation of digital highways, engines and network for the entire healthcare ecosystem to operate efficiently.

    As the system is heavy and challenge is hard, we all have to contribute the best in us - skills, knowledge, experiences, resources. This is what our company's name manifests, this is what we are about.

    David Vakhtangishvili

    Founder and CEO
